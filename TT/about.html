Our Story:

Justin Husson is a filmmaker from Toronto and Jasmine Duke is a stay at home mom from London, Ontario. Each year we throw a massive Halloween Party, and in 2019 we plan to take it to the next level and make our own haunted trail! Soon after, Christina Husson, Justin's mom, who runs several small businesses signed on to create the final trio. We are looking for some help in our funding to make the haunt as amazing as possible. There are some things we could use some help achieving such as costumes, props, makeup and security.

The Story Of Ravyn Graves:

Ravyn was born to an evil witch and a demon. Ravyn was taught the magical arts and evil rituals and left at her cabin. While she slept, the people from the nearby town came and locked her in her cabin and set it on fire. Ravyn woke up in horror as she burned to death, she yelled out a magical curse on those who scorned her. The next day, they all died in their sleep from a mysterious illness, the entire town considered plagued and never to be visited again. Legend has it that the ghost of Ravyn still haunts the woods and raises the zombies of the townspeople to kill any intruders to her forest.
